About me

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at Université de Haute-Alsace, working at the IRIMAS Laboratory. My research focuses on deep learning for time series classification, with an interest in model efficiency, ensemble learning and adaptive methods. I aim to develop lightweight and robust models that can be applied in areas such as healthcare, robotics and real-time systems.

Keywords: data science, computer vision, pattern recognition, machine learning, time series, human motion, deep learning.
